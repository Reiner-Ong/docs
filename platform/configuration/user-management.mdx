## Your company (Tenant)

Your company is the **Tenant** — the top-level account that contains everything in LiftAuth. All lifts, jobs, invoices, and team members belong to your Tenant.

Inside your Tenant, there are three types of people:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Full control over the account. Manages billing, team members, and all settings. Every company has one Owner.\
    \
  </Card>

  <Card title="Admin" icon="briefcase">
    Manages day-to-day operations — scheduling jobs, reviewing jobsheets, approving and sending invoices. Cannot change ownership settings.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Technician" icon="wrench">
    Receives jobs on their phone and carries out the work on-site. Cannot access invoices or team settings.\
    \
    \
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## \
Building Managers

A **Building Manager** is the company or person responsible for a building you service. They are external to your company and do not need a LiftAuth account.

<Card title=" " icon="building">
  Each Building Manager is linked to one or more **lifts**. When a job is completed on one of their lifts, they receive a jobsheet to sign and an invoice to pay — both delivered by email.
</Card>

<Tip>
  One Building Manager can be responsible for multiple buildings, each with multiple lifts.
</Tip>

LiftAuth runs in your web browser — no software to install. Open **Google Chrome**, **Safari**, or **Microsoft Edge** and go to your company's LiftAuth dashboard link.

<Tip>
  Bookmark the dashboard so you can find it quickly each day.
</Tip>

## Creating a new account

There are two ways you may be getting started with LiftAuth:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="wallet-cards" href="#if-you-received-an-invitation">
    ## You received an invitation

    Your Owner or Admin has sent you an invite by email or SMS. Follow the link in the message to set up your account.\\

         
  </Card>

  <Card icon="building" href="#if-you-are-setting-up-a-new-company">
    ## You are setting up a new company

    You are the first person from your company to use LiftAuth. You will create your company account and become the Owner.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### If you received an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation or sign-up page">
    If you were invited by your admin, check your email or SMS for a message from LiftAuth and click the link inside.

    If you are creating a new account, go to the LiftAuth web dashboard and click **Create an account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Choose to sign in with your **email** or **phone number**, then enter your name and contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    LiftAuth will send a 6-digit code to your email or phone. Enter the code to confirm it's you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join your organisation">
    You will see a screen showing your pending invitations. Click **Join** next to your organisation to accept. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your account is ready. You will be taken to the dashboard automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Invitation links expire after 7 days. If your link has expired, ask your Owner or another Admin to [resend the invitation](/admin/managing-your-team).
</Warning>

### If you are setting up a new company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the LiftAuth sign-up page">
    Open the LiftAuth dashboard link and select **Create a new account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your name, email address, and phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity">
    LiftAuth will send a 6-digit one-time code to your email or phone. Enter the code to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your company">
    Enter your company name and address. This creates your Tenant — the account that all your team members and lifts will belong to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your account and company are ready. You are now the Owner of your LiftAuth account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing in after your account is created

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email or phone number">
    Type the email address or phone number linked to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request your one-time password (OTP)">
    Click **Send code**. LiftAuth will send a 6-digit code to your email or phone by SMS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type the 6-digit code and click **Verify**. The code expires after 10 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You will be taken to your dashboard. If you belong to more than one company, you will be asked to choose which one to open.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you do not receive the code within 2 minutes, check your spam folder (for email) or confirm your phone number is correct. Click **Resend** to get a new code.
</Warning>

## Signing out

Click your name in the bottom-left corner, then select **Sign out**

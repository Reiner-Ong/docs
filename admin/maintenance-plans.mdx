A **maintenance plan** is the automation layer of LiftAuth. Once set up for a lift, it automatically creates a maintenance job and a draft invoice on a fixed basis — you just review and close them.

## What a maintenance plan does

Before setting up a plan, make sure you have already:

- [Registered the lift](/admin/lifts)
- [Added the building manager](/admin/building-managers) and assigned them to the lift

## Creating a maintenance plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lift">
    Go to **Lifts** and click on the lift you want to set up a plan for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Maintenance Plans tab">
    Inside the lift detail page, click the **Maintenance Plans** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create plan">
    Click **Create maintenance plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the frequency">
    Choose how often the lift needs a service:

    - **Monthly** — once every month
    - **Quarterly** — once every 3 months
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the preferred schedule">
    Pick a date and LiftAuth will schedule the closest working day
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up the invoice template">
    Add the line items and amounts that will appear on each automatically generated invoice.

    Set **Jobs per invoice** based on how often you want to invoice the Building Manager:

    | Maintenance frequency | Invoice frequency | Jobs per invoice |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | Monthly | Monthly | 1 |
    | Monthly | Quarterly | 3 |
    | Monthly | Annually | 12 |
    | Quarterly | Quarterly | 1 |
    | Quarterly | Half-yearly | 2 |
    | Quarterly | Annually | 4 |

    An invoice draft is automatically generated once the required number of jobs in a cycle have been closed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The plan is now active and LiftAuth will create the first job based on your schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What is automated vs. what you still do manually

| LiftAuth handles automatically | You still need to do |
| --- | --- |
| Creating the maintenance job each cycle | Closing the completed job after it is signed |
| Generating a draft invoice after the job closes | Reviewing and approving the draft invoice |
| Sending the jobsheet signing link |  |

<Tip>
  Auto-created jobs will appear in the **Jobs** list. Just assign a technician and confirm the scheduled time — everything else flows automatically.
</Tip>

## Editing a plan

1. Open the lift's detail page.
2. Go to the **Maintenance Plans** tab and click on the plan.
3. Click **Edit**, make your changes, and save.

<Tip>
  Changes to a plan take effect from the next cycle — they will not affect jobs that have already been created.
</Tip>

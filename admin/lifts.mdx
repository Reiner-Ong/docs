---
title: "Managing Lifts"
description: "How to register, find, and manage the lifts in your system."
---

A **lift** in LiftAuth represents one physical elevator unit at a building. Every lift must be registered through the **BCA LEAP portal** — this is how LiftAuth pulls the lift's official equipment\
details automatically.

## What is the BCA LEAP portal?

The BCA (Building and Construction Authority) LEAP portal is the Singapore government's database of all registered lifts. Every lift has its own page on this portal with its equipment ID, address, and maintenance details.

To register a lift in LiftAuth, you need the URL of that lift's page on the BCA LEAP portal. It looks like this:

[https://www2.bca.gov.sg/leap/ptoenquiry/xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx](https://www2.bca.gov.sg/leap/ptoenquiry/xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx)

## Adding a new lift

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the lift on BCA LEAP">
    Go to the [BCA LEAP portal](https://www2.bca.gov.sg/leap/PTOEnquiry/Search), search for the lift, and open its PTO Enquiry page. Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Lifts in LiftAuth">
    Click **Lifts** in the left sidebar, then click **Add lift**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the BCA URL">
    Paste the URL you copied into the BCA PTO Enquiry URL field and click **Look up & continue**. LiftAuth will automatically pull the equipment ID, address, and other details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the remaining details">
    Confirm or correct the pre-filled details, select the lift type, and assign a building manager if you have one ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register the lift">
    Click **Register lift**. The lift is now in your system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also add a lift by scanning the QR code on the lift's equipment plate using the LiftAuth mobile app — this retrieves the same BCA URL automatically.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  If a lift is already registered in your account, LiftAuth will notify you and prevent a duplicate from being created.
</Warning>

## Assigning a building manager to a lift

If you did not assign a building manager when registering the lift, you can do it later:

1. Open the lift's detail page.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Select the building manager and click **Save**.

<Warning>
  A building manager must be assigned to a lift before you can create jobs or maintenance plans for it.
</Warning>

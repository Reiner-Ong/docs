A **maintenance record** (also called a jobsheet) is the report created when a technician completes a job. It contains the checklist results, photos, technician notes, and signatures from both the technician and the building manager.

## Reading a record

Inside a record you will find:

**Checklist results** — each item is shown as Pass or Fail. Failed items will have technician notes and photos attached.

**Photos** — click any photo thumbnail to view it full size.

**Signatures** — the technician's signature and the building manager's signature. A record is not complete until both are collected.

## Downloading the PDF

1. Open the record.
2. Click **Download PDF** on the right.
3. The signed jobsheet will download to your computer.

<Tip>
  The PDF is the official document for compliance purposes. Keep a copy according to your record-keeping requirements.
</Tip>

## Sending a signing link {#sending-a-signing-link}

If the building manager was not on-site to sign, there are two ways to get them the signing link as an admin off-site.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="wallet-cards">
    ## Send directly

    LiftAuth emails the link automatically to the building manager's primary contact. Open the record and click **Send signing link** — no further input needed.
  </Card>

  <Card icon="link">
    ## Copy the link

    LiftAuth generates the link and copies it to your clipboard. You can then share it yourself via WhatsApp, SMS, or any other method.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  If a building manager has more than one contact, the email is always sent to the **first contact** on their record. Make sure the primary contact is the right person to receive signing requests.
</Warning>

Once the building manager clicks the link and signs, the record status updates to **Signed** automatically and the job appears in your Inbox ready to close. No LiftAuth account is needed on their end.

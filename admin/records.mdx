A maintenance record is the report generated when a technician completes a job. It documents what was done and serves as the report the Building Manager receives once the job is finished.

<Warning>
  Once a record has been made, it cannot be changed.
</Warning>

## How to Sign a Record {#sending-a-signing-link}

If the building manager was not on-site to sign, there are two ways to get them the signing link as an admin off-site.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="wallet-cards">
    ## Send directly

    LiftAuth emails the link automatically to the building manager's primary contact. Open the record and click **Send signing link** — no further input needed.
  </Card>

  <Card icon="link">
    ## Copy the link

    LiftAuth generates the link and copies it to your clipboard. You can then share it yourself via WhatsApp, SMS, or any other method.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Once the building manager clicks the link and signs, the record status updates to **Signed** automatically and the job appears in your Inbox ready to close. No LiftAuth account is needed on their end.

---
title: "Scanning a QR Code"
description: "How to use the QR code scanner in the LiftAuth mobile app."
---

The Scan tab opens your camera to read **BCA PTO QR codes** — the QR codes printed on a lift's compliance plate.

<Warning>
  The scanner only works with BCA PTO QR codes. It will not recognise other types of QR codes.
</Warning>

## How to scan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Scan tab">
    Tap the **Scan** tab at the bottom of the screen. Your camera will open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Point your camera at the QR code">
    Hold your phone steady, with the QR code inside the white frame. The app scans automatically — no button to press.

    If the code won't scan, tap **Enter URL** at the top right to paste the BCA PTO URL manually instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the result">
    The app looks up the lift in the BCA system. What happens next depends on whether the lift is already registered in LiftAuth:

    | Result | What happens |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Lift already registered** | Opens the lift's details page immediately |
    | **Lift not yet registered** | Shows the lift details from BCA — tap **Register Lift** to add it to LiftAuth |
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the scan fails, make sure there is good lighting and the QR code is clean. Try moving slightly closer or further away.
</Tip>

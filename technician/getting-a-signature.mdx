---
title: "Getting a Signature"
description: "How to get the building manager's signature after completing a job."
---

Every completed job needs to be signed by both you and the building manager. LiftAuth automatically sends the building manager a signing request by email and SMS as soon as you submit your work.

## Signing on your phone

If the building manager is on-site, they can sign directly on your phone.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the completed job">
    Tap the job and go to the signature screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hand your phone to the building manager">
    They draw their signature on the screen and enter their name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You sign next">
    Once they are done, you draw your own signature to complete the sign-off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing via email link

If the building manager is not on-site, they will receive an email and SMS with a link to sign remotely. You do not need to do anything — LiftAuth sends this automatically.

<Tip>
  If the building manager says they did not receive the email, ask your admin to resend the signing link from the [Maintenance Records](/admin/records#sending-a-signing-link) page.
</Tip>

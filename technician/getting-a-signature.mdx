---
title: "Getting a Signature"
description: "How to get the building manager's signature after completing a job."
---

After you submit your work, the building manager must sign the record. You have two options.

## Option 1 — Sign on your phone

If the building manager is on-site, they can sign directly on your phone.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Sign on device">
    On the signature screen, tap **Sign on device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hand your phone to the building manager">
    They enter their name and draw their signature on the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Submit signature">
    The record is signed and the job moves to **Signed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Option 2 — Send a signing link

If the building manager is not on-site, you can send them a link to sign remotely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Share under Signature link">
    On the signature screen, tap **Share** to send the link by email or SMS, or tap the link to copy it manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap I've shared the link">
    Tap on **Share Link**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The building manager will receive the link and can sign on their own device. The job moves to **Signed** automatically once they complete it.

<Tip>
  If the building manager did not receive the link, ask your admin to resend it from the [Records](/admin/records#sending-a-signing-link) page.
</Tip>

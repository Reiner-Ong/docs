---
title: "Create an Adhoc Job"
description: "How to create an unscheduled job from the mobile app."
---

If you need to log a visit that was not already scheduled — such as a last-minute emergency — you can create a job directly from the app.

## Finding the lift {#finding-a-lift}

You can get to a lift's page in two ways: 

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Browse the Lifts tab" icon="list">
    Tap the **Lifts** tab at the bottom of the screen and tap the lift from the list.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Scan the QR code" icon="qr-code">
    Tap the **Scan** tab and point your camera at the BCA PTO QR code on the lift's compliance plate.

    - If the lift is already registered, it opens the lift's detail page directly.
    - If the lift is not yet registered, you will be prompted to add it to LiftAuth before creating the job.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Scanning the QR code is the fastest way to find the right lift when you are already on-site.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  The scanner only works with BCA PTO QR codes. If the code won't scan, tap **Enter URL** at the top right to paste the BCA PTO URL manually.
</Warning>

## Creating the job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lift detail page">
    Find the lift using either method above and tap it to open its detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the job type">
    Tap **Create Job** and choose the type:

    - **Maintenance job** — routine scheduled visit
    - **Breakdown job** — lift is out of service
    - **Repair job** — specific component work
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter any required details, such as linked issues for a Repair job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save**. The job is created and you can start work immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

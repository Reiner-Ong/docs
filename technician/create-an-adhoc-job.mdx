---
title: "Create an Adhoc Job"
description: "How to create a new job from the mobile app."
---

If you need to log a visit that was not already scheduled, or perhaps a last minute emergency job, you can create a job directly from the app.

## How to create a job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the lift">
    Go to the **Lifts** tab and tap the lift that needs a visit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create Job">
    On the lift's detail page, tap **Create Job** and select the job type — Maintenance, Breakdown, or Repair.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter any required details for the job, such as linked issues for a Repair job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save**. The job is now created and you can start work immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the lift you need is not in your list, contact your admin — you may not have access to it yet.
</Tip>

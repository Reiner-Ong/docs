---
title: "Completing a Job"
description: "How to complete a maintenance, breakdown, or repair job in the LiftAuth mobile app."
---

Once you have [started a job](/technician/starting-a-job), the steps depend on the job type. All three types end with [getting a signature](/technician/getting-a-signature).

## Maintenance — completing the checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Work through each checklist item">
    For each item, tap to check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes and photos for failed items">
    If an item fails, add a note explaining what is wrong. [Attach a photo](/technician/completing-a-job#attaching-photos) if you can. You can also [raise an issue](/technician/reporting-a-fault)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign as the technician">
    Scroll to the bottom of the checklist. You will see a **Technician Sign-off** section. Draw your signature and tap **Submit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the checklist">
    When all items are done, tap **Submit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Do not skip checklist items. Every item must be marked Checked before you can submit.
</Warning>

## Breakdown — recording the fault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe what happened">
    Write a clear description of the fault and what you did to address it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach photos">
    [Attach photos](/technician/completing-a-job#attaching-photos) of the fault and any work done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign as the technician">
    Scroll to the bottom of the checklist. You will see a **Technician Sign-off** section. Draw your signature and tap **Submit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Submit** when you are done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Repair — recording outcomes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go through each listed issue">
    For each fault you were sent to fix, record the outcome:

    - \*\*Resolved \*\*— the fault has been resolved
    - \*\*Not Resolved \*\*— you could not fix it this visit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes and photos">
    Add a note for each outcome and [attach photos](/technician/completing-a-job#attaching-photos) where relevant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign as the technician">
    Scroll to the bottom of the checklist. You will see a **Technician Sign-off** section. Draw your signature and tap **Submit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Submit** when you are done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Attaching photos {#attaching-photos}

You can attach photos to any checklist item, fault description, or issue outcome.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the camera icon">
    Tap the camera icon next to the item you want to add a photo to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take or choose a photo">
    Take a new photo with your camera or choose one from your gallery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit. Get close enough so the fault is clearly visible.
</Tip>

## What happens after you submit

Once you submit, a record is created and you will be taken to the signing screen. The Building Manager must sign before the job is complete — [see how to get a signature](/technician/getting-a-signature).

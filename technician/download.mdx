LiftAuth has a mobile app for technicians. You will use this app to view your jobs, complete checklists, and capture signatures on-site.

## Downloading the app

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="apple">
    ## iPhone 

    Open the **App Store**, search for **LiftAuth**, and tap **Get**.\
    (Coming soon!)
  </Card>

  <Card icon="android">
    ## Android

    Open the **Google Play Store**, search for **LiftAuth**, and tap **Install**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Logging in for the first time

You will need the phone number or email address that your admin used to invite you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Tap the LiftAuth icon on your phone to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your phone number or email">
    Type the phone number or email address your admin used when they invited you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    LiftAuth will send a 6-digit code to your phone by SMS or to your email. Type the code into the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You will be taken to your home screen where you can see your jobs for today.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you cannot log in, contact your admin to make sure your phone number or email has been added to the system.
</Warning>

## Selecting your company

If you work for more than one company, you will be asked to choose which one to open after logging in. Tap the company name to select it.

## Logging out

1. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap **Log out** and confirm.

## Creating an account

If you are new to LiftAuth and do not have an account yet, tap **Create account** on the login screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your name">
    Type your full name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email or phone number">
    Choose whether to register with your **email** or **phone number**, then enter your details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    LiftAuth will send a 6-digit code to your email or phone. Type the code into the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your account is ready. You will be taken to your home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your admin has already added you to the system, use the same email or phone number they used — this links your account to your company automatically.
</Tip>

## Logging in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Tap the LiftAuth icon on your phone to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your phone number or email">
    Type the phone number or email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    LiftAuth will send a 6-digit code to your phone by SMS or to your email. Type the code into the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You will be taken to your home screen where you can see your jobs for today.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you cannot log in, contact your admin to make sure your phone number or email has been added to the system.
</Warning>

## Selecting your company

If you work for more than one company, you will be asked to choose which one to open after logging in. Tap the company name to select it.

## Logging out

1. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap **Log out** and confirm.

---
title: "Reporting a Fault"
description: "How to report a problem you find on a lift."
---

If you notice something wrong with a lift — even if it is not part of your scheduled job — you can report it directly from the app. Your admin will see it in their Inbox.

## How to report a fault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lift">
    Go to the **Lifts** tab and tap the lift where you found the problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Report Issue">
    On the lift's detail page, tap **Report Issue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the severity">
    Choose how serious the problem is:

    | Severity | Use when |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Critical** | The lift is a safety risk — stop using it immediately |
    | **Major** | The lift still works but has an operational problem |
    | **Minor** | Cosmetic or minor issue that does not affect operation |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the fault">
    Write a short description of what you found. Be specific — include what you saw, heard, or felt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a photo (recommended)">
    Tap the camera icon to attach a photo. A photo helps your admin understand the problem without needing to visit the site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Submit**. The fault is saved and your admin is notified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you select **Critical**, your admin will be alerted immediately. Do not leave a lift in service if you believe it is unsafe.
</Warning>

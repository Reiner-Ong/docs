title: "Starting a Prescheduled Job"
description: "How to open and start a job in the LiftAuth mobile app."
---

Before you begin any work on-site, open the job in LiftAuth so your work is recorded correctly.

If you need to start and Ad-hov Job follow the instructions here

## Finding your job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Jobs tab">
    Tap **Jobs** at the bottom of the screen and tap your job on the calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the details">
    Confirm the building address, lift, and job type are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Start">
    When you are on-site and ready, tap **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link any issues (optional)">
    A preparation screen will appear. If there are known faults on this lift, you can link them to this job here. Linked issues can be marked as resolved or not resolved when you complete the work.

    When ready, tap **Start Checklist** (or **Start Report** for breakdown and repair jobs).

    [Learn what happens to linked issues after you submit →](/technician/completing-a-job#after-submit)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Can't find your job? Check the **Pending** tab or [scan the QR code](/technician/create-an-adhoc-job#finding-a-lift) on a job printout to open it directly.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  If the job details look wrong — wrong building, wrong lift, or wrong job type — do not start the job. Contact your admin before proceeding.
</Warning>

## What happens next

Once you start, LiftAuth will guide you through the work depending on the job type:

- **Maintenance** — you will work through a checklist of items to inspect
- **Breakdown** — you will record what went wrong and what you did
- **Repair** — you will record the outcome for each known fault

[Learn how to complete a job →](/technician/completing-a-job)

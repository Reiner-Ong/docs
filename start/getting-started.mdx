LiftAuth is a lift maintenance management platform built for Singaporean lift servicing companies. It helps you schedule service visits, record completed work, track faults, and send invoices — all in one place.

## Role-based access

<div style={{ marginTop: "-1rem", marginBottom: "-1rem" }}>
  | Role           | What they can do                                                                                         |
  | -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
  | **Owner**      | Full access — manage the team, billing, invoices, and all settings.<br />Also has Technician access.     |
  | **Admin**      | Manage jobs, invoices, and team members. Cannot change billing.<br />Also has Technician access.         |
  | **Technician** | Mobile app only. Can only see jobs assigned to them. Cannot access invoices, payments, or team settings. |
</div>

<Tip>
  Every company starts with one Owner. The Owner can then invite additional Admins and Technicians from the Team page.
</Tip>

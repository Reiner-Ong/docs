LiftAuth is a lift maintenance management platform built for Singaporean lift servicing companies. It helps you schedule service visits, record completed work, track faults, and send invoices — all in one place.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Admins" icon="monitor">
    Office staff who plan and oversee operations from the web dashboard.\\
  </Card>

  <Card title="Technicians" icon="wrench">
    Field workers who carry out jobs on-site using the mobile app.\
    \\
  </Card>

  <Card title="Building Managers" icon="building">
    Responsible for a building. Receive records to sign via email — no login required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

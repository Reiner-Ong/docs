  <Card title="For Technicians" icon="tablet-smartphone" href="/technician/download">

  </Card>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A lift is registered and a maintenance plan is set up">
    An admin adds the lift to the system and schedules how often it needs servicing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A job is automatically created when a service is due">
    LiftAuth creates the job and notifies the assigned technician on their phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The technician visits the site and completes the checklist">
    All inspection results, notes, and photos are recorded in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The building manager signs the jobsheet">
    They sign directly on the technician's phone, or via a link sent to their email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The admin reviews and sends the invoice">
    LiftAuth generates the invoice automatically. The admin approves it and sends it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Building managers do not need a LiftAuth account. They receive a link by email to sign the jobsheet.
</Tip>

---
title: "What is LiftAuth?"
description: "LiftAuth helps lift maintenance companies keep track of lifts, schedule service visits, record completed work, and send invoices"
---

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="For Admins" icon="monitor" href="/admin/logging-in">
    Use the **web dashboard** (on any computer) to schedule jobs, manage invoices, track faults, and oversee your team.
  </Card>

  <Card title="For Technicians" icon="tablet-smartphone" href="/technician/download">
    Use the **LiftAuth mobile app** (on your phone) to view your jobs, complete checklists, and capture signatures on-site.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Building managers do not need a LiftAuth account. They receive a link by email to sign the jobsheet.
</Tip>

## What appears in the Inbox

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="circle-check">
    ## Jobs awaiting close

    A technician has finished their work and the building manager has signed off. You need to close the job to complete it. [Learn how to close a job →](/admin/jobs#closing-a-job)
  </Card>

  <Card icon="newspaper">
    ## Draft invoices to review

    An invoice has been automatically generated and is waiting for your approval before it is sent. [Learn how to approve an invoice →](/admin/invoices#approving-a-draft-invoice)
  </Card>

  <Card icon="rocket">
    ## Critical issues

    A technician has reported a safety fault on a lift. These need urgent attention. [Learn how to handle an issue →](/admin/issues#responding-to-a-critical-issue)
  </Card>

  <Card icon="hourglass">
    ## Overdue jobs

    A job was scheduled but not completed on time. [Learn how to reschedule a job →](/admin/jobs#rescheduling-a-job)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Start your day here. Once your Inbox is clear, everything is up to date.
</Tip>

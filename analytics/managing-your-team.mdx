---
title: "Managing Your Team"
description: " How to invite, manage, and remove team members in LiftAuth."
---

The **Team** page is where you manage who has access to your LiftAuth account. You can invite new staff, change roles, and suspend people who no longer work with you.

<Warning>
  Only **Owners** and **Admins** can manage team members.
</Warning>

## Inviting a new team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team">
    Click **Team** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click the **Invite member** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their contact details">
    Enter the person's email address or phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose their role">
    Select the role that matches what they need to do:

    - **Admin** — full access to the web dashboard including jobs, invoices, and team management
    - **Technician** — mobile app access only; can only see their assigned jobs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send invite**. The person will receive an email or SMS with a link to set up their account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Invitation links expire after 7 days. If a team member does not accept in time, resend the invitation from the Team page.
</Warning>

## Changing someone's role

1. Open the **Team** page and find the team member.
2. Click on their name.
3. Click **Change role**, select the new role, and save.

## Managing pending invitations

If someone has been invited but has not yet accepted, their entry will show as **Pending**. You can **cancel** the invitation from the Team page if you change your mind.

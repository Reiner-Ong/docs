---

## Adding a new building manager

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Building Managers">
    Click **Building Managers** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add">
    Click the **Add building manager** button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the company details">
    Fill in the company name and street address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a contact person">
    Enter the contact's name, email address, and phone number. You can add more than one Contact to a Building Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The building manager now appears in your list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Double-check the email address — this is where invoices and signing requests are sent.
</Warning>

## Editing a building manager

1. Click on the building manager in the list.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Make your changes and click **Save**.

## Archiving a building manager

Archiving removes a building manager from your active list. It does **not** delete them — all their past jobs, jobsheets, and invoices are kept on record. You would archive a building manager if you no longer service any of their buildings.

1. Open the building manager's page.
2. Click **Archive**.
3. Confirm when prompted.
